About AstroAtlas

AstroAtlas is an innovative, interactive, and educational web-based platform designed to explore key astronomical events that occurred on a specific date in history—whether a planetary discovery, satellite launch, or milestone in astrophysics. Acting as a digital time machine, the platform invites users to journey through the vast history of space science, one day at a time.

Our Missions

Deep Space Listening

Scanning the vast skies using advanced technology to detect potential extraterrestrial signals and cosmic phenomena.

Planetary Research

Studying planetary systems, environments, atmospheres, geological structures,and potential habitability to understand their formation and evolution.

Education

A simplified approach to complex space science education for students and young individuals.

Life Long Learning

Users are presented with significant astronomical events tied to that calendar date in past years. Blending scientific facts with creative storytelling.
